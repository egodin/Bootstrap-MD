Summary

Ward Hunt Island is located near the northern coastline of Ellesmere Island, Nunavut, in the Canadian High Arctic The station is 50 m from a coastal lagoon on the northern side of the island in a polar desert landscape This record contains air temperature data within the period 1 January 2019 to 31 December 2020 (links to the full AWS database are given in the metadata and landscape images looking outwards from the station in summer

Station Code: WHIB

Name: Ward Hunt Island Observatory, Canada

Location: 83 0914 N, 74 1328 W altitude 8 m

Data time interval: 1/1/2019 to 31/12/2020
